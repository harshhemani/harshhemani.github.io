Accelerating Deep Neural Network Training (Part 1)

[ COLAB NOTEBOOK ]
This notebook investigates the challenges of accelerating deep learning through parallel training and large batch sizes. While distributing mini-batches across machines speeds up computation, it increases the effective batch size, which often degrades model convergence and final accuracy.
The notebook demonstrates that while increasing batch sizes (from 32 up to 4096) significantly reduces training time per epoch, it leads to a clear drop in test-set accuracy on the MNIST dataset. The notebook found that scaling the learning rate by the square root of the batch size ratio (batchsizenew​/batchsizeold​​) offered the best stability and performance for this specific benchmark.
